1. Introduction

OMGSERVERS respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal data of developers using our services. This Privacy Notice explains how we collect, use, and protect information related to your use of our platform.

2. Information We Collect

OMGSERVERS only collects data necessary for service operation. We do not collect personal information from players who use games hosted on our platform.

Data Collected from Developers:

  • Account Information: When registering, you provide an email address. Additionally, developers may be asked to complete a survey containing questions aimed at better understanding user needs.
  • Payment Information: We do not directly store payment details. Payments are processed through third-party providers.
  • Usage Data: We collect non-personal logs related to service interactions (e.g., API requests, deployed Docker images, and resource usage).

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Provisioning: Managing accounts, authenticating users, and providing infrastructure for game servers.
  • Security and Fraud Prevention: Detecting unauthorized access, preventing abuse, and ensuring platform stability.
  • Billing and Payments: Processing service payments via third-party payment providers.
  • Improvement of Services: Analyzing usage trends to enhance platform performance.

4. Data Sharing

OMGSERVERS does not sell or share personal data with third parties except in the following cases:

  • Legal Compliance: If required by law, we may disclose data to regulatory authorities.
  • Service Providers: We use external payment processors and cloud service providers to facilitate payments and infrastructure operations.

5. Data Retention

  • Account Information: Retained as long as the account is active. If a developer deletes their account, data is removed except for records required for legal or accounting purposes.
  • Usage Logs: Retained for internal analysis and security monitoring. Logs are anonymized where possible.

  • Restricted access to administrative systems.
